Our cryptocurrency
is like no other.

It is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange
that uses strong cryptography to secure financial transactions,
control the creation of additional
units, and verify the transfer of assets. Cryptocurrencies use decentralized control as
opposed to centralized digital currency and central banking systems.

The decentralized control of each cryptocurrency works through distributed ledger technology, typically a blockchain,
that serves as a public financial transaction database.
